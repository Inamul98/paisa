Install Paisa on your home screen for quick access — works like a native app, no App Store needed.
How to install on iPhone / iPad:
1 Tap the Share button ⎙ at the bottom of Safari
2 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3 Tap "Add" — Paisa will appear on your home screen
⚠️ Must be opened in Safari — Chrome on iOS does not support installation.
